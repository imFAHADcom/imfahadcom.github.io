The Wealthiest Countries Expel Their Poor

Published on: February 09, 2025

Individuals from low-wage countries can migrate to higher-wage nations, work there until retirement, and then return to their home countries after saving enough to buy a house and secure a decent life.

But has anyone considered the lowest-paid workers in high-income countries?

These individuals often struggle to buy a home or secure a comfortable life in their own countries due to inflation and rising costs. As a result, when they retire, they have only one option: permanent migration to low-cost countries, where they can achieve a better standard of living and own a home that ensures them a stable life.
